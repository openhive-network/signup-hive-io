Why so many different options?

Account creation is not free on Hive. It requires the creator to pay a small fee to create an account. The account address on Hive is an easy-to-remember username from 3 to 16 characters.

You can choose a username when you create your account. That's why Hive charges a small fee to discourage users from claiming many usernames.

Some registrars create accounts for free to make onboarding easier while others charge small fee. Free account creation services rely on some sort of verification to mitigate the abuse.

There are also projects available that charge the account creation fee and create accounts without taking information like an email from the user. The paid services are valuable because of the privacy they provide.
